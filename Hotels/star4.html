Washington Opera Hotel Paris features quality-lodging facilities in a Louis XV style building. Whether it is watching a show at Opera-Comique or checking the stock market at Bourse, guests of this charming hotel are just a few steps away.

Villa Royale Montsouris Paris is a Spanish Moroccan style decorated property in Paris offering accommodation for all types of travellers. Guests staying at this hotel can easily visit Pantheon, an example of Neoclassicism, with a facade modelled on the Pantheon in Rome.
