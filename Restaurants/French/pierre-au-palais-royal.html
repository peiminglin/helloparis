PIERRE AU PALAIS ROYAL

10, rue de Richelieu ou 7, rue Montpensier - Paris 1er

Good-humoured "bistronomical," traditional cuisine and a fine selection of wines are to be found at Pierre au Palais Royal, located in the 1st district. This 60-year-old restaurant, taken over in September 2007 by acclaimed sommelier Eric Sertour and talented chef Pascal Bataillé. Enjoying a perfect location near the Comédie Française and the Théâtre du Palais-Royal, it is also a renowned post-show venue with its clever after 11PM supper menu (last orders at midnight).
