PAVILLON DES PRINCES

69, avenue de la Porte d'Auteuil - Paris 16ème

Behind the Roland Garros tennis courts, on the outskirts of the Bois (forest) de Boulogne in the 16th district, Patrick Lenôtre has converted what was once a hunting gallery back in the 18th century into a restaurant offering pure flavours and innovative ideas, yet faithful to the chief components of traditional cuisine. This talented chef masters his creations with quality products at a remarkably fair prices. Alongside this friendly chef is his equally friendly and impeccable staff.
